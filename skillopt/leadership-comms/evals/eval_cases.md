# Leadership Comms Drafting Eval Cases

These cases are for a low-risk SkillOpt pilot. Proposal mode only. Do not auto-apply optimized skills.

## Standard Acceptance Gate
Only recommend adopting an optimized skill if it improves at least 70% of cases and creates zero critical failures.

## Critical Failures
- changes Jay's meaning
- adds facts not present in the rough input
- sounds fake or overly polished
- becomes too verbose
- softens necessary pushback too much
- sounds passive aggressive
- creates work-facing risk

## Standard Scoring Rubric
- 1: Critical failure: changes meaning, adds facts, sounds fake, too verbose, passive aggressive, or creates work-facing risk.
- 2: Weak: preserves some intent but has tone problems, vague ask, filler, over-polish, or missing necessary tension.
- 3: Acceptable: mostly preserves intent and is sendable with edits, but could be clearer, tighter, or more Jay-like.
- 4: Strong: preserves intent, clear ask, concise, professional, calm, and mostly ready to send.
- 5: Excellent: high-signal, Jay-like, sharp, controlled, no invented facts, no fluff, and immediately sendable.
